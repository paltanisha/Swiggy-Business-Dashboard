# Swiggy Business Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Business Dashboard developed in Microsoft Excel to analyze 12,000 simulated Swiggy food delivery orders. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of business performance by involving key metrics related to revenue, restaurant performance, customer satisfaction, payment preferences, and delivery operations. The objective is to transform raw transactional data into valuable insights that support data-driven business decisions.

🎯 Objective

The dashboard was designed to answer the following business questions:

- Which cities generate the highest revenue?
- Which cuisines contribute the most to total sales?
- Which restaurants are the top revenue performers?
- How does weather affect delivery time?
- What are the most preferred customer payment methods?
- What is the overall customer satisfaction level?
- How efficiently is the delivery network performing?
- What key operational insights can help improve business performance?

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Excel Formulas (IF, INDEX-MATCH, COUNTIF, SUM, AVERAGE, TEXT)
- KPI Dashboard Design
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence Reporting

📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following business metrics:

- Total Orders
- Gross Merchandise Value (GMV)
- Swiggy Revenue
- Average Customer Rating
- Average Delivery Time
- Cancellation Rate

📈 Key Insights

- Hyderabad emerged as the highest revenue-generating city.
- Fast Food was the top-performing cuisine category.
- Burger King generated the highest restaurant revenue among all listed restaurants.
- Rainy weather resulted in the longest average delivery time.
- Digital payment methods accounted for the majority of customer transactions.
- The platform maintained a high average customer rating of 4.8/5, indicating strong customer satisfaction.

💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were identified:

- Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing cities to maximize revenue.
- Expand partnerships with top-performing restaurant brands.
- Improve delivery fleet allocation during adverse weather conditions.
- Continue promoting digital payment incentives to enhance customer retention.
- Strengthen investment in high-demand cuisine categories such as Fast Food.
